5. Behavior Summary

Quick requirements snapshot for the player lane.

Player is an overlay, not a top-level route.
Back order: chooser → expanded info → player exit.
Live TV and Series support sibling switching without exit.
Movies keep up-next secondary, not louder than primary playback.
Chooser overlays stay contextual and separate.
Visual direction: Google TV layout clarity, Apple TV restraint, Netflix/YouTube information hierarchy.
