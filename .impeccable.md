# Design Context: CrispyTivi

## Users
Designed for IPTV power-users and media consumers interacting primarily via TV screens, projectors, and living-room monitors. They are typically sitting at a distance (10-foot UI) and using directional inputs (remote, gamepad, keyboard) rather than a mouse or touch screen. Their primary job is to discover and watch live television and VOD content instantaneously.

## Brand Personality
Cinematic. Functional. Effortless.

## Aesthetic Direction
"Cinematic Utility" — A dark-mode, glassmorphic hybrid of Google TV (clean, pill-shaped navigation, Material iconography), TiviMate (hyper-dense but readable EPG grids), and Netflix (edge-to-edge backdrop art, dark gradients, immersive presentation).

**Anti-references:** Must not look like a generic, bloated smart TV launcher (e.g., standard Samsung Tizen or basic Android TV). It must not feel like a sluggish web app or require a precise mouse pointer.

## Design Principles & Tokens

1. **D-Pad First Navigation:** Every interactive element must have a stark, obvious focus state (Pure White `#FFFFFF`) and be flawlessly reachable via directional keys (Up/Down/Left/Right).
2. **The 3-Layer Architecture:**
   - **Layer 0 (Canvas):** Edge-to-edge playing video or ken-burns effect key art.
   - **Layer 1 (Glass):** UI is a transparent glass layer (`rgba(10, 10, 12, 0.65)` with deep background blur) floating over the canvas.
   - **Layer 2 (OSD):** Minimalist controls that appear instantly and fade out after 3 seconds of inactivity.
3. **Typography for TVs:** 
   - **Headings/Hero:** Fluid, massive, editorial `Outfit` font (`64px`+).
   - **Data Density (EPG):** Crisp, ultra-legible `Inter` or `Google Sans` (`14px` - `16px`).
4. **Color Palette:**
   - **Primary Text:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.95)`
   - **Secondary/Metadata Text:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.60)`
   - **Surface Cards:** `rgba(28, 28, 32, 0.8)` with a `1px rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` border.
   - **Brand Accent:** Cinematic gradient "Crispy Orange/Red" (`#FF4B2B` to `#FF416C`) used sparingly for progress bars and live badges.
5. **Iconography:**
   - Google **Material Symbols Rounded** (`24px`-`28px`).
   - Default is Outlined (`FILL: 0`), focused/active state is Filled (`FILL: 1`).
6. **Instancy & Flow (Animations/Layout):**
   - Global top navigation rail hides when scrolling down, returns when scrolling up.
   - Hero sections take up 70% of the screen above content lanes.
   - Player seek bar is a razor-thin bottom line that expands upward only when focused.
   - EPG grids run with a top-right PiP (Picture-in-Picture) mini-player or a deeply blurred background stream.
7. **Accessible Contrast:** High-contrast text on dark surfaces with clear hierarchy, supporting reduced-motion environments.
